Macro Definition Documentation

#define SWITCH_CHECK_WAIT_MS   (5)

Amount of that is waited between to checks.

A higher value leads to better performs (less checks and thus cpu cycles), but also increases the possibility to lose a HIGH / LOW change.

#define SWITCH_PRESS_DOUBLE_MS   (500)

Maximum amount of time between to pressed to be recognized as a double press.

#define SWITCH_PRESS_DOUBLE_TIMES   (SWITCH_PRESS_DOUBLE_MS / SWITCH_CHECK_WAIT_MS)

Calculated amount of times the checks have to be repeated.

#define SWITCH_PRESS_POWER_MS   (2000)

Minimum amount of time the switch has to be pressed for POWER function.

#define SWITCH_PRESS_POWER_TIMES   (SWITCH_PRESS_POWER_MS / SWITCH_CHECK_WAIT_MS)
